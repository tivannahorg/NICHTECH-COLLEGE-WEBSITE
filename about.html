About Us

Nichtech Technologies Training College is a TEVETA accredited institution located in Choma, Adastra area near Mize Lodge.

The college is committed to providing practical, hands-on training that equips students with real-world skills for employment and entrepreneurship.


Our Mission

To empower students with quality technical and vocational skills that meet industry standards and promote innovation.

Our Vision

To become a leading training institution in Zambia, producing competent and skilled graduates ready to transform society.
